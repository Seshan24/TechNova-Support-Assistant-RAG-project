# Warranty FAQ

## What does the standard warranty cover?
The TechNova standard 1-year limited warranty covers **manufacturing defects** in materials and workmanship under normal use. This includes:
- Hardware failures not caused by the user
- Battery defects (battery holds less than 80% of original capacity within the warranty period)
- Display defects (dead pixels, ghosting, backlight failure)

## What is NOT covered?
- Physical damage (drops, cracks, dents)
- Liquid damage (unless the product is rated water-resistant and used within those limits)
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications
- Cosmetic wear and tear
- Lost or stolen devices

## How do I claim warranty service?
1. Visit technova.example.com/warranty
2. Enter your serial number and order details
3. Describe the issue with photos if possible
4. We will email you a return authorization (RMA) within 1 business day

## What is NovaCare+?
NovaCare+ is our **extended protection plan**. It covers:
- 2 additional years of warranty (3 years total)
- 2 incidents of accidental damage per year (small service fee per incident)
- 24/7 priority phone support
- Free battery replacement once the battery drops below 80% capacity

You can add NovaCare+ within **60 days** of your original purchase. Pricing varies by product.

## My device broke just after the warranty expired. Can you help?
Contact support — we sometimes offer **goodwill repairs** at a reduced cost, especially for issues that were borderline cases during the warranty period. No guarantees, but always ask.

## Is the warranty transferable if I sell the device?
Yes, the warranty follows the device, not the buyer, as long as the serial number is registered.
