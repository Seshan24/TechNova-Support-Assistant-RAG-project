# Troubleshooting FAQ

## My Nova Phone X1 won't turn on
1. **Charge it** with the original 65W charger for at least 30 minutes. If you see a charging icon, the device is alive — just wait for it to power on.
2. **Force restart:** Press and hold the Power + Volume Down buttons simultaneously for 10 seconds.
3. If still unresponsive, contact support — this may be a hardware issue covered by warranty.

## My Nova Laptop Pro 14 has a black screen
1. Connect the charger and check the indicator light. If it's not on, try a different USB-C port or cable.
2. Force restart: Press and hold the Power button for **10 seconds**.
3. If the keyboard backlight is on but the screen is black, try connecting an external monitor to confirm whether the issue is the display or the system.

## My Nova Earbuds 2 won't connect to my phone
1. Put both earbuds in the case and close the lid for 10 seconds.
2. Open the lid, press and hold the button on the back of the case for 8 seconds until the LED flashes white.
3. On your phone, forget the existing pairing under Bluetooth settings, then pair again.

## My Nova Watch Series 3 isn't tracking my heart rate
1. Make sure the watch is snug on your wrist (not loose, not over a tattoo, and the back sensor is in contact with skin).
2. Check that "Heart Rate" is enabled in **Settings > Health**.
3. Clean the sensor on the back with a soft, slightly damp cloth.
4. Restart the watch (hold the side button for 10 seconds).

## My Nova Tablet 11 is running slowly
1. Check available storage. Tablets below 5% free storage will slow down dramatically.
2. Close background apps in the recent apps view.
3. Restart the tablet.
4. If it persists, **Settings > System > Reset > Factory Reset** as a last resort (back up first).

## My Nova Sound speaker can't hear my voice commands
1. Make sure the microphone is not muted (LED indicator on top is white when muted).
2. Move the speaker away from walls and sources of background noise.
3. Re-train NovaVoice in the Nova Home app under **Voice > Re-train**.

## How do I check my battery health?
- **Phone & Tablet:** Settings > Battery > Battery Health
- **Laptop:** Settings > System > Battery (shows cycle count and current max capacity)
- **Watch:** Settings > Battery > Battery Health
- **Earbuds:** Open the Nova Audio app on your phone

## What if none of this works?
Contact support at support@technova.example.com with:
- Your product serial number
- A description of what you tried
- A photo or screen recording if applicable

We aim to respond within **4 business hours**.
