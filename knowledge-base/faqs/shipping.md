# Shipping FAQ

## How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping within the continental US takes **3-5 business days**. Express shipping takes **1-2 business days**. International shipping varies by country, typically **7-14 business days**.

## How much does shipping cost?
- **Standard shipping:** Free on orders over $50; otherwise $5.99
- **Express shipping:** $14.99 flat rate
- **International shipping:** Calculated at checkout based on destination

## Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 60 countries. Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.

## Can I track my order?
Yes. Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order from your TechNova account dashboard under "My Orders."

## My tracking says delivered but I didn't receive my package. What do I do?
First, check with neighbors and your building's front desk. If it's still missing after 48 hours, contact support at support@technova.example.com with your order number. We will open a claim with the carrier.

## Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?
Only if the order has not yet shipped. Contact support immediately. Once an order has been handed to the carrier, we cannot reroute it.

## Do you offer same-day delivery?
Same-day delivery is available in select metro areas (NYC, LA, SF, Chicago, Seattle) for orders placed before 11am local time. Surcharge: $24.99.
