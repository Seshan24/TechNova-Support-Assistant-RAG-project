# Trade-In Program FAQ

## What is the TechNova Trade-In program?
You can send us any old device — from TechNova or another brand — and receive **store credit** that can be applied immediately to a new purchase.

## What devices can I trade in?
- Smartphones (any brand, less than 7 years old)
- Tablets (any brand, less than 7 years old)
- Laptops (any brand, less than 5 years old)
- Smartwatches (Nova devices only, less than 5 years old)
- Wireless earbuds (Nova devices only)

## How much will I get?
Trade-in values depend on:
- Model and year
- Cosmetic condition (Like New, Good, Fair, Poor)
- Functional condition (does it power on, screen condition, battery health)

Get an **instant estimate** at technova.example.com/trade-in. Final offers are confirmed after we receive and inspect the device.

## What if my device has a cracked screen?
Devices with cracked screens are still accepted but at the **Fair** or **Poor** valuation. We can often offer at least 30% of the Like New value.

## Is the trade-in offer guaranteed?
The instant estimate is **valid for 14 days**. If you accept, we send a prepaid shipping label. After we inspect the device, you'll receive the final offer. If it matches the estimate, your store credit is issued immediately. If we revise the offer downward, you can accept or have your device returned at no cost.

## What if my device is rejected?
We will ship it back to you at our expense. No questions asked.

## What happens to my data?
We **professionally wipe** all devices using DOD 5220.22-M-compliant data erasure. However, you should always perform a factory reset and unlink your accounts **before** shipping.

## Can I use trade-in credit on multiple orders?
Yes. Trade-in credit is added to your account as store credit and can be used across multiple orders. It does not expire.

## Environmental impact
All devices we receive are either refurbished and resold (~70%) or responsibly recycled (~30%). In 2023, the trade-in program kept over **24 metric tons** of e-waste out of landfills.
