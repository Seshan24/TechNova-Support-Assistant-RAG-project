# Account FAQ

## How do I create a TechNova account?
Visit technova.example.com/signup and enter your email and a password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.

## I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and enter your email. A reset link will be sent. The link is valid for **30 minutes**.

## How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
Go to **Account Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication**. You can choose:
- SMS code (less secure but easy)
- Authenticator app (recommended)
- Hardware security key (FIDO2)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA on accounts linked to high-value purchases.

## How do I update my email address?
Go to **Account Settings > Profile > Email**. You'll need to verify the new email via a confirmation link. The old email will receive a notification.

## How do I delete my account?
Go to **Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account**. This is permanent and will:
- Delete all your personal data after 30 days
- Cancel any active subscriptions
- Forfeit any unused store credit or rewards points

Order history is retained for 7 years for legal/tax reasons but is anonymized.

## My account was locked. Why?
Common reasons:
- Too many failed login attempts (auto-unlocks after 30 minutes)
- Suspicious activity detected (contact support)
- A chargeback was filed on a previous order
- Violation of terms of service

## Can I have multiple accounts?
Yes, but each must have a unique email address. Sharing accounts between people is discouraged and may trigger security flags.
