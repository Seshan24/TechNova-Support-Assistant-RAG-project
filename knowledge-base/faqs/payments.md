# Payments FAQ

## What payment methods do you accept?
- Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay and Google Pay
- NovaPay (our own wallet, only on technova.example.com)
- Klarna and Affirm for installment plans (US only, qualifying orders)
- Bank wire transfer (business orders over $5,000 only)

## Can I split a payment across two cards?
Not on a single order. As a workaround, you can purchase a TechNova gift card with one card and use it together with another card at checkout.

## Do you offer financing or installment plans?
Yes. On orders **$500 and above** in the US, you can choose **Klarna** (4 interest-free installments) or **Affirm** (3-, 6-, or 12-month plans with APR shown at checkout). Both involve a soft credit check that does not affect your score.

## When is my card charged?
Your card is **authorized** at order placement but only **charged when the order ships**. For pre-orders, the card is charged when the product becomes available.

## Why was my payment declined?
Common reasons:
- Billing address doesn't match the address on file with your bank
- Insufficient funds or credit limit reached
- Bank's fraud detection flagged the transaction (call your bank)
- Country mismatch (using a foreign card on a US site)

## Are sales tax and VAT included in the price?
- **US:** Sales tax is added at checkout based on your shipping address.
- **EU:** All prices include VAT.
- **UK:** All prices include VAT.
- **Other countries:** Prices shown are pre-tax; customs duties may apply on delivery.

## Is my payment information secure?
Yes. TechNova does **not** store your full card number. Payments are processed by PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant processors (Stripe and PayPal). Your card details never touch our servers.

## Can I pay with cryptocurrency?
Not at this time.
