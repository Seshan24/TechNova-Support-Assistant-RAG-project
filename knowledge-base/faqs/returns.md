# Returns FAQ

## What is your return window?
TechNova offers a **30-day return window** for most products, starting from the date of delivery.

## How do I start a return?
1. Log in to your TechNova account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click "Return Item" next to the relevant order
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Drop the package off at any authorized carrier location

## Do I have to pay for return shipping?
For most returns, **return shipping is free** if you use the prepaid label we provide. If the return is due to buyer's remorse (i.e., the product works fine but you changed your mind), a $9.99 restocking fee may be deducted from your refund.

## What condition must the product be in?
- Original packaging included
- All accessories included (cables, adapters, manuals)
- Product in like-new condition with no physical damage
- Software/account unlinked (for phones, laptops, and smartwatches)

## Are there any non-returnable items?
Yes:
- Earbuds and earphones, once the seal has been broken (for hygiene reasons)
- Customized or engraved products
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Gift cards

## How long does a refund take?
Once we receive your return, processing takes **3-5 business days**. After that, refunds appear on your original payment method within **5-10 business days** depending on your bank.

## Can I exchange instead of refunding?
Yes. Choose "Exchange" instead of "Return" in the returns flow. You'll get a credit equal to the purchase price that you can apply to a new order immediately.
