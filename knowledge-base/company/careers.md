# Careers at TechNova

## Why TechNova?

We're a 2,400-person company building consumer hardware and software loved in over 60 countries. We move faster than the giants and care more about craft than the startups.

## Our Values

1. **Build for humans** — Engineering elegance is only meaningful when real people benefit.
2. **Sustainability is non-negotiable** — Our products outlast trends. So should our planet.
3. **Honesty in marketing** — We never advertise a feature that doesn't ship.
4. **Diverse teams, better products** — We hire from every background, geography, and life path.

## Where We Hire

- **Austin, TX (HQ)** — most engineering, product, design, ops
- **Dublin, IE** — EMEA operations, customer support, regulatory
- **Singapore** — APAC operations and supply chain
- **Mexico City, MX** — LATAM operations and customer support
- **Remote** — select roles in engineering, design, and content

## Benefits

- Competitive base salary + annual performance bonus
- Equity (RSUs) for all full-time employees
- 100% employer-paid health, dental, vision insurance (US)
- **Unlimited PTO** (with a 4-week minimum mandatory)
- 16 weeks paid parental leave for all parents
- $2,000/year learning budget
- Employee discount: 30% off all Nova products
- 401(k) with 6% match (US)

## How to Apply

Visit technova.example.com/careers, browse open roles, and apply through our portal. We respond to every application within **14 days**, even if the answer is no.

## Diversity & Inclusion

- **47%** of TechNova employees identify as women or non-binary (industry avg: 28%)
- **38%** of leadership roles are held by people from historically underrepresented backgrounds
- We publish a full DEI report annually at technova.example.com/dei

## Internships

Summer internships open in November for the following June–August. Both undergraduate and graduate students may apply. We hired **120 interns** in 2024, with a 73% return-or-full-time-offer rate.
