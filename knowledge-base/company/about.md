# About TechNova

## Who We Are
TechNova is a consumer electronics company founded in **2018** and headquartered in **Austin, Texas**. We design and manufacture premium smartphones, laptops, smartwatches, and audio products under the **Nova** brand.

## Our Mission
> "To build technology that empowers everyday creators — without breaking the bank or the planet."

## By the Numbers
- **2,400** employees worldwide
- **4** product lines (Phone, Laptop, Watch, Earbuds)
- **60+** countries served
- **>5 million** devices sold since founding

## Sustainability Commitments
- All packaging is **100% recyclable** as of 2023
- Carbon neutral operations target: **2027**
- Trade-in program offers credit for old devices regardless of brand
- All NovaOS software updates supported for **5 years** minimum, reducing forced upgrades
