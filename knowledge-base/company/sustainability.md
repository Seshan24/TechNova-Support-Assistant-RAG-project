# TechNova Sustainability Report (2024)

## Our Climate Goals

- **2027:** Carbon-neutral global operations (Scope 1 + Scope 2)
- **2030:** Carbon-neutral entire supply chain (Scope 3)
- **2035:** 100% recycled or renewable materials in all products

## 2024 Progress

### Manufacturing
- **62%** of materials in 2024 product lineup were recycled or renewable (target was 60%)
- 100% of aluminum used in Nova Laptop Pro 14 enclosures is recycled
- Nova Watch Series 3 Stainless Steel cases now use 80% recycled steel

### Packaging
- **100% of retail packaging** is now plastic-free (achieved 2023)
- All inks used are soy-based
- Product boxes are sized 12% smaller on average vs 2023, reducing shipping emissions

### Energy
- **84%** of TechNova-owned facilities are powered by renewable energy
- All 4 regional offices are 100% renewable
- New Austin HQ is LEED Platinum certified

### E-Waste
- Trade-in program kept **24 metric tons** of e-waste out of landfills in 2024
- ~70% of returned devices are refurbished and resold
- ~30% are responsibly recycled via certified e-waste partners

### Software Longevity
- All Nova devices receive software updates for at least **5 years** post-launch
- This reduces forced upgrades and extends product lifespans

## Independent Verification

Our climate disclosures are verified annually by **Bureau Veritas** under the ISO 14064 standard. Full reports are available at technova.example.com/sustainability.

## Awards
- "Most Sustainable Tech Company" — GreenTech Awards 2023
- "B Corp Certified" — 2024 (score: 112.4)
- Top quartile in CDP Climate Change disclosure (2023, 2024)

## Where We Need to Improve
We are honest about where we fall short:
- Scope 3 supplier emissions remain our biggest challenge
- Battery recycling at end-of-life is still industry-wide < 50% effective
- We are exploring closed-loop lithium recovery with a 2026 pilot
