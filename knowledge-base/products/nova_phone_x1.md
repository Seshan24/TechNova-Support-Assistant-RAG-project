# Nova Phone X1

## Overview
The Nova Phone X1 is TechNova's flagship smartphone, launched in March 2024. It is designed for power users who need long battery life, a premium camera, and fast performance.

## Key Specifications
- **Display:** 6.7-inch OLED, 120Hz refresh rate, 2400x1080 resolution
- **Processor:** NovaCore A9 (8-core, 3.2GHz)
- **RAM:** 12GB
- **Storage:** 256GB or 512GB (no SD card slot)
- **Battery:** 5000mAh with 65W fast charging
- **Camera:** 50MP main + 12MP ultra-wide + 10MP telephoto (3x optical zoom)
- **Front camera:** 32MP
- **Operating System:** NovaOS 5 (based on Android 14)
- **Connectivity:** 5G, Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, USB-C
- **Water resistance:** IP68
- **Weight:** 198g
