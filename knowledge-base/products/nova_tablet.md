# Nova Tablet 11

## Overview
The Nova Tablet 11 is TechNova's mid-range tablet, designed for students, casual creators, and households who want a versatile second screen. Launched November 2024.

## Key Specifications
- **Display:** 11-inch IPS LCD, 2560x1600 resolution, 90Hz refresh rate
- **Processor:** NovaCore A7 (8-core, 2.6GHz)
- **RAM:** 8GB
- **Storage:** 128GB or 256GB (microSD up to 1TB)
- **Battery:** 8000mAh, up to 12 hours mixed use
- **Charging:** 30W USB-C
- **Cameras:** 13MP rear, 8MP front
- **Speakers:** Quad-speaker setup, Dolby Atmos
- **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, optional LTE
- **Stylus support:** Nova Pen 2 (sold separately, $99)
- **Operating System:** NovaOS Tab 3
- **Weight:** 510g
