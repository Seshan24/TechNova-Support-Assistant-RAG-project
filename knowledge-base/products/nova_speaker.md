# Nova Sound Mini & Nova Sound Max

## Overview
TechNova's two smart speakers — the compact **Nova Sound Mini** and the premium **Nova Sound Max** — bring high-fidelity audio and voice assistant capabilities to the home.

## Nova Sound Mini

### Specifications
- **Drivers:** 1x 1.6-inch full-range
- **Power output:** 8W
- **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.2, AirSync
- **Voice assistant:** Built-in NovaVoice
- **Microphones:** 3 far-field
- **Weight:** 340g
- **Dimensions:** 98mm diameter x 84mm tall

### Pricing
- $79

### Colors
- Charcoal, Cream, Coral

## Nova Sound Max

### Specifications
- **Drivers:** 1x 4-inch woofer, 2x 0.5-inch tweeters
- **Power output:** 90W
- **Room sensing:** Automatic acoustic calibration
- **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3, AirSync, Apple AirPlay 2
- **Voice assistant:** Built-in NovaVoice
- **Microphones:** 6 far-field with beamforming
- **Stereo pairing:** Yes (two Max units)
- **Multi-room:** Yes, with any Nova Sound device
- **Weight:** 2.5kg
- **Dimensions:** 168mm diameter x 215mm tall

### Pricing
- $299

### Colors
- Charcoal, Cream

## In the Box (both models)
- Speaker
- Power cable (1.5m)
- Quick start guide

## Compatibility
Both speakers can be controlled via the Nova Home app (Android and iOS) and respond to NovaVoice commands. The Max additionally supports Apple HomeKit.

## Warranty
1-year limited warranty.
