# Nova Earbuds 2

## Overview
The Nova Earbuds 2 are TechNova's second-generation wireless earbuds, featuring active noise cancellation, spatial audio, and excellent battery life.

## Key Specifications
- **Driver:** 11mm dynamic driver
- **Noise cancellation:** Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) with Transparency Mode
- **Battery life:** 7 hours (earbuds) + 28 hours total with charging case
- **Charging:** USB-C and Qi wireless
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth 5.3, multipoint pairing (2 devices)
- **Water resistance:** IPX4 (earbuds), IPX2 (case)
- **Codecs:** SBC, AAC, LDAC, NovaLossless
- **Weight:** 5.4g per earbud, 48g case
