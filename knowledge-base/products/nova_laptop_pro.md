# Nova Laptop Pro 14

## Overview
The Nova Laptop Pro 14 is TechNova's premium laptop, aimed at creators, developers, and professionals who need serious performance in a portable form factor.

## Key Specifications
- **Display:** 14-inch Mini-LED, 3024x1964 resolution, 120Hz, 1000 nits peak brightness
- **Processor:** NovaCore M4 Pro (12-core CPU, 18-core GPU)
- **RAM:** 16GB unified memory (configurable up to 64GB)
- **Storage:** 512GB SSD (configurable up to 4TB)
- **Battery:** Up to 18 hours video playback
- **Ports:** 3x Thunderbolt 4, 1x HDMI 2.1, 1x SD card reader, 3.5mm headphone jack
- **Webcam:** 1080p with TechNova Smart Framing
- **Keyboard:** Backlit, full-size, with Touch ID
- **Weight:** 1.55 kg
- **Operating System:** NovaOS Desktop 12
