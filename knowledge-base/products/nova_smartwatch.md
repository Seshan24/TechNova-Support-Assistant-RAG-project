# Nova Watch Series 3

## Overview
The Nova Watch Series 3 is TechNova's smartwatch focused on health tracking, fitness, and seamless integration with the Nova Phone X1.

## Key Specifications
- **Display:** 1.9-inch AMOLED, always-on
- **Sizes:** 42mm and 46mm cases
- **Materials:** Aluminum or Stainless Steel
- **Battery life:** Up to 36 hours (standard), up to 60 hours (low-power mode)
- **Charging:** Magnetic wireless charging
- **Water resistance:** 5 ATM (suitable for swimming)
- **Health sensors:** Heart rate, SpO2, ECG, skin temperature, sleep tracking
- **Fitness:** GPS, accelerometer, gyroscope, altimeter
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth 5.3, Wi-Fi, optional LTE (cellular model)
- **Operating System:** NovaWatch OS 4
