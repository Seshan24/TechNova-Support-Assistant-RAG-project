# NovaOS 5.2 Release Notes

**Release date:** November 4, 2024
**Affected devices:** Nova Phone X1
**Update size:** 1.9 GB

## What's New

### AI Features
- **NovaAssist** — a new on-device AI assistant with private, local processing for most queries
- Smart Reply suggestions in Messages and email
- AI-powered photo cleanup tool (remove objects or people from photos)

### Cross-Device
- New **Continuity** feature lets you start a task on your Nova Phone X1 and continue on a Nova Laptop Pro 14 or Nova Tablet 11
- Universal clipboard across all logged-in Nova devices
- AirSync improvements for faster file transfers (now up to 3x faster on Wi-Fi 6E)

### Accessibility
- New **Voice Control** mode lets you operate the phone entirely by voice
- Increased text size options up to 4x
- Improved screen reader support for third-party apps

### Bug Fixes
- Fixed a critical bug where the alarm could fail to ring if the phone was in Do Not Disturb mode
- Fixed a memory leak in the Camera app causing slowdowns after extended use
- Fixed an issue where 5G could remain inactive after switching SIMs

## Important Notes
- **NovaAssist requires NovaOS 5.2 or later** and is currently available in English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese
- Older accessories may need a firmware update for full compatibility — check the Nova Home app

## Security
- 18 CVEs patched, including 2 rated **Critical**

## How to Install
Settings > System > Software Update > Check for Update
