# NovaWatch OS 4.1 Release Notes

**Release date:** September 9, 2024
**Affected device:** Nova Watch Series 3
**Update size:** 380 MB

## What's New

### Health & Fitness
- **Cycle Tracking** with predictive insights and irregular cycle alerts
- New **Mindfulness** app with guided breathing and reflection sessions
- VO2 Max estimation now works during cycling, not just running
- Sleep tracking improvements: REM detection accuracy up by ~20%

### Workouts
- New workout types: **Pilates**, **HIIT Cooldown**, **Tai Chi**
- Auto-pause for running and cycling
- Custom workout creator — chain together intervals and rest periods

### Watch Faces
- 6 new watch faces including Modular Plus, Photo Stack, and a minimal Astronomy face
- Complication support expanded for third-party developers

### Connectivity
- Improved Wi-Fi connection stability when away from your phone
- LTE models: better signal acquisition in low-coverage areas

## Bug Fixes
- Fixed an issue where heart rate could occasionally report 0 BPM during workouts
- Fixed a crash when using the timer with very long durations (> 24 hours)
- Improved water-eject functionality after swims

## Security
- 4 CVEs patched

## How to Install
Open the Nova Watch app on your Nova Phone X1 > My Watch > General > Software Update

Your watch must be:
- On its charger
- Charged to at least 50%
- Within Bluetooth range of your paired phone
