# Warranty Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

## 1. Standard Limited Warranty

TechNova provides a **1-year limited warranty** on all hardware products against defects in materials and workmanship under normal use, beginning on the date of original retail purchase.

## 2. What Is Covered

- Manufacturing defects
- Premature hardware failure under normal use
- Battery that falls below **80% of original capacity** within the warranty period (verified by TechNova diagnostic tools)
- Display defects including dead pixels exceeding 3 individual pixels or any cluster of 2 adjacent dead pixels
- Audio output failure
- Connectivity hardware failure (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular modems)

## 3. What Is NOT Covered

- Cosmetic damage including scratches, dents, and worn finishes
- Damage caused by accident, abuse, misuse, fire, earthquake, or other external causes
- Damage caused by operating the product outside its published specifications (e.g., exposing an IPX4 product to submersion)
- Damage from unauthorized service or modification
- Consumable parts (replaceable ear tips, watch bands) unless defective on arrival
- Lost or stolen devices
- Software issues, including data loss

## 4. NovaCare+ Extended Protection

NovaCare+ is an **optional paid extension** that adds:

- **2 additional years** of warranty coverage (3 years total)
- **Up to 2 accidental damage incidents per year**, subject to a service fee per incident:
  - Phone/Earbuds/Watch screen repair: $29
  - Other accidental damage: $99
- **24/7 priority technical support**
- **Free battery replacement** once battery health drops below 80%

NovaCare+ must be purchased **within 60 days** of the original product purchase.

## 5. How to Make a Warranty Claim

1. Visit `technova.example.com/warranty`
2. Enter your product serial number and proof of purchase
3. Describe the issue and provide photos/videos if applicable
4. Receive an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) within **1 business day**
5. Ship the product using the prepaid label provided
6. Receive a repaired unit, replacement unit, or refund within **10 business days** of receipt

## 6. Replacement vs Repair

TechNova reserves the right to either repair the original product, replace it with a new or refurbished unit of equal value, or issue a refund. Replacement units carry the **remainder of the original warranty** or 90 days, whichever is longer.
