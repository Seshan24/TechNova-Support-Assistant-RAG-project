# Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

## 1. Processing Time

Orders are processed within **1 business day** of payment confirmation. Orders placed after 2pm CT or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.

## 2. Shipping Options & Costs

### Domestic (United States)
| Method | Delivery time | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 3–5 business days | Free over $50; $5.99 otherwise |
| Express | 1–2 business days | $14.99 |
| Same-day (select metros) | Same day if ordered by 11am local | $24.99 |

### International
- **Canada:** 4–7 business days, calculated at checkout
- **EU, UK:** 5–10 business days, calculated at checkout
- **Asia-Pacific:** 7–14 business days, calculated at checkout
- **Other countries:** 10–21 business days, calculated at checkout

## 3. Customs & Duties

For international orders, customers are responsible for:
- Import duties
- VAT or local sales tax (where not already included)
- Any other fees imposed by the destination country

These are charged by the carrier at delivery, not by TechNova.

## 4. Carriers

TechNova uses **UPS**, **FedEx**, and **DHL** for outbound shipments. Domestic orders may also ship via **USPS** for items under 2lbs.

## 5. Tracking

A tracking number is emailed to the customer when the order ships. Tracking is also visible in the customer's account under "My Orders."

## 6. Lost or Stolen Packages

- **Mis-delivery (marked delivered but missing):** Wait 48 hours, then contact support. We will file a carrier claim.
- **Confirmed lost in transit:** TechNova will reship the order at no cost.
- **Stolen after delivery:** Customer should file a police report. TechNova will offer goodwill replacements case-by-case but is not obligated.

## 7. P.O. Boxes & APO/FPO Addresses

We can ship small items (earbuds, accessories) to P.O. Boxes. Larger items (laptops, tablets) require a physical street address. APO/FPO addresses are supported via USPS only.

## 8. Restricted Destinations

Due to local regulations, we cannot ship to:
- Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria
- Russia and Belarus (suspended since 2022)
- Any U.S. sanctions list jurisdictions
