# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

## 1. Information We Collect

TechNova collects the following categories of information:

- **Account information:** Name, email address, password (hashed), phone number
- **Order information:** Shipping address, billing address, payment method (tokenized), purchase history
- **Device information:** Serial numbers of registered products, software version, diagnostic logs (if opted in)
- **Usage information:** Site interactions, support ticket history, marketing preferences

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To fulfill orders and provide customer support
- To honor warranty claims
- To send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping notifications)
- To send marketing emails (only with your opt-in consent)
- To improve products and services based on aggregated, anonymized usage data
- To comply with legal obligations including tax and consumer protection laws

## 3. Information Sharing

TechNova does **NOT sell** personal information to third parties.

We share data with:
- Shipping carriers (UPS, FedEx, DHL) for order delivery
- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) for transaction handling
- Cloud infrastructure providers (under strict data processing agreements)
- Law enforcement when required by valid legal process

## 4. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Receive your data in a portable format
- Withdraw marketing consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email privacy@technova.example.com.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active account data:** Retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Personal data deleted within 30 days
- **Order records:** Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance (anonymized after account deletion)
- **Support tickets:** Retained for 2 years

## 6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses essential cookies (required for the site to function) and optional analytics cookies (only with your consent). You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest), regular security audits, and strict access controls. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you within **72 hours**.

## 8. Children

TechNova services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 9. Contact

Data Protection Officer: privacy@technova.example.com
