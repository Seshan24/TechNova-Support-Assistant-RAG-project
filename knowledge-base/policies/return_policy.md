# Return Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

## 1. Return Window

TechNova provides a **30-day return window** for most products, beginning on the date of delivery as recorded by the shipping carrier.

For **NovaCare+ members**, the return window is extended to **45 days**.

## 2. Eligibility

To be eligible for return, the product must be:

- In **like-new condition**
- Returned with **all original packaging** and accessories (cables, chargers, adapters, manuals, free gifts)
- Free of any physical or liquid damage
- Properly unlinked from any user accounts (factory reset for phones, watches, and laptops)

## 3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Earbuds and earphones once the hygienic seal has been broken
- Custom-engraved or personalized products
- Digital products including software licenses and subscriptions
- Gift cards (digital or physical)
- Items marked "Final Sale" at the time of purchase

## 4. Restocking Fees

| Return Reason | Fee |
|---|---|
| Defective product | None |
| Wrong item sent by TechNova | None |
| Buyer's remorse (changed mind) | $9.99 |
| Opened laptop without defect | 10% of purchase price |

## 5. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**. We cannot refund to a different card or account.

- Credit/debit card refunds: 5-10 business days after processing
- PayPal refunds: 1-3 business days
- Store credit refunds: Immediate

## 6. Exchanges

Customers may exchange items within the return window. An exchange credit is issued immediately upon return acceptance and can be used toward a new order. Price differences will be charged or refunded as appropriate.

## 7. International Returns

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any customs duties. We recommend using a tracked shipping service.
