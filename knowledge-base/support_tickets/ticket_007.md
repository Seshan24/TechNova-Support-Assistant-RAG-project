# Support Ticket #007

**Status:** Resolved
**Date opened:** 2024-10-11
**Date closed:** 2024-10-12
**Product:** Nova Sound Max
**Category:** Connectivity

## Customer Issue
Customer's Nova Sound Max worked fine for music but would not respond to NovaVoice commands. Microphone LED indicator was off.

## Diagnosis
Microphone had been muted via the physical button on top of the speaker. Customer did not realize the LED showing white meant "muted" (a common UX confusion).

## Resolution
Walked customer through pressing the mute button once to unmute. Microphone LED turned off and NovaVoice resumed working.

## Notes
This is the **11th ticket this quarter** with the same root cause. The UX convention is reversed from many competitors (LED on = muted vs LED on = active). Product team is evaluating whether to flip the indicator behavior in the next firmware update or add an in-app explanation.
