# Support Ticket #001

**Status:** Resolved
**Date opened:** 2024-04-12
**Date closed:** 2024-04-14
**Product:** Nova Phone X1
**Category:** Battery

## Customer Issue
Customer reported that their Nova Phone X1 battery was draining unusually fast — from 100% to 20% in about 4 hours of normal use, just 2 weeks after purchase.

## Diagnosis
Remote diagnostic confirmed battery health at 67% — well below expected. This is a manufacturing defect covered under standard warranty.

## Resolution
Issued RMA. Customer mailed the device back using the prepaid label. A replacement Nova Phone X1 (same model, color, and storage) was shipped within 3 business days.

## Notes
This appears to be part of a small batch of defective batteries from week 14 of 2024 production. Engineering team has been notified.
