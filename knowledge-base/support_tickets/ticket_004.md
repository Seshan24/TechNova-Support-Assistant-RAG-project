# Support Ticket #004

**Status:** Resolved
**Date opened:** 2024-07-08
**Date closed:** 2024-07-09
**Product:** Nova Watch Series 3
**Category:** Warranty

## Customer Issue
Customer reported that their 8-month-old Nova Watch Series 3 (46mm Stainless Steel, LTE) had a cracked screen after a "small bump against a doorframe."

## Diagnosis
Cracked glass is classified as **physical damage**, which is NOT covered under the standard 1-year warranty. Customer did NOT have NovaCare+.

## Resolution
Informed customer the repair would be out-of-pocket at $189 for the 46mm Steel model. Customer accepted and shipped the watch in for repair. Repair completed in 4 business days.

Offered the customer NovaCare+ for the repaired watch (within 60-day post-repair window) — customer purchased it for future peace of mind.

## Notes
Highlight NovaCare+ more prominently at checkout for higher-priced models. The $29 screen-repair fee under NovaCare+ would have saved this customer $160.
