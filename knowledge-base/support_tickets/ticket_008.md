# Support Ticket #008

**Status:** Resolved
**Date opened:** 2024-11-05
**Date closed:** 2024-11-19
**Product:** Nova Laptop Pro 14
**Category:** International Warranty

## Customer Issue
Customer purchased a Nova Laptop Pro 14 in the US in March 2024, then relocated to Germany. The laptop developed a keyboard fault in November. The customer attempted to file a warranty claim from Germany but was told the US warranty was "not honored" by the EU support team.

## Diagnosis
**This was incorrect.** TechNova provides an **International Limited Warranty** on all laptops. The EU support rep was misinformed.

## Resolution
US-based support escalated to the EU regional manager. The customer was issued an RMA, shipped the laptop to TechNova's Dublin service center, and received a replacement keyboard repair within 8 business days. All shipping costs were covered.

Issued a **goodwill $100 store credit** for the misinformation and inconvenience.

## Notes
**Action item for support training:** All laptops carry international warranty. Phones and watches do not, due to regional regulatory differences. Update the internal support knowledge base and re-train EU and APAC teams.
